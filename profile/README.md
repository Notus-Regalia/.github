## About

Notus Regalia is a technology company that builds operational systems and the
ventures that run on them. Founded in 2021 and headquartered in the Philippines,
it operates as a founder-operator platform: a shared engineering backbone of APIs,
data pipelines, and security infrastructure, reused across the companies it builds,
backs, and incubates.

## Sectors

| Sector | Focus |
|---|---|
| Government & Civic Operations | Secure, accountable systems for public institutions |
| Defense | Mission-grade systems for resilience, security, and operational advantage |
| Healthcare | Connected care delivery, data, and infrastructure |
| Enterprise | Shared platforms that scale across the venture portfolio |
| Energy | Grid intelligence through industrial monitoring and capture |

## Operating Model

- A single technical backbone serving every sector and venture
- Subsidiaries, portfolio companies, and early-stage startups under one platform
- Long-term ownership and institutional governance
